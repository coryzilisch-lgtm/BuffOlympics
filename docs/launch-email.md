## Plain-text version

Hey everyone,

The **Buff Olympics — The Herd Games** app is live! This is your home base for
everything on **August 14, 2026** — signing up, following the schedule, and tracking
the day.

**Open it here: [APP LINK]**

Tip: on your phone, tap "Add to Home Screen" — it installs like a real app so you can
jump back in with one tap.

**Getting started (2 minutes):**
1. Create an account with your email and a password.
2. Pick your tribe — **Buffalo** or **Texas Roadhouse**.
3. Claim your games (see below).

**Sign up now — before it locks:**
- **Claim your game time-slots.** Browse the games and grab the slots you want.
  Buffalo players can claim up to **4** slots; Texas Roadhouse up to **2**.
- **Dip Off:** cooking? Add yourself to the Dip Off (5 cooks per tribe).
- **Relay:** claim one relay leg for your tribe.
- **Song request:** drop your song for the DJ while you're in there.

⏰ **Sign-ups lock the day before game day.** After that the lineup is set, so get
your slots in early.

**On game day, the app becomes your guide:**
- 📅 **Your schedule** — your games woven into the day's timeline so you know where to
  be and when.
- 🔎 **Hidden idol clues** — clues unlock throughout the day. Keep checking; find one
  and it's worth points for your tribe.
- 🍲 **Vote in the Dip Off** — once voting opens, cast your one vote for the best dip.
- 📊 **Scores** — follow along as referees log results. Team totals stay **sealed**
  until the big reveal at the Closing Ceremony.

Refs — you'll get a separate note with your **join code** to set up your ref account.

See you out there. Go get some points for the herd.

— [YOUR NAME]
